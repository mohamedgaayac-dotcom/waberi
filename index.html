About School

Taariikhda Schoolka

Schoolkaan waxaa dhidibada loo taagay September 2007 asagoo soo maray marxalado kala duwan. Dugsiga markii hore waxa la oran jiray School Waaberi, taasoo markii dambe loo badalay SHEIKH MOHAMUD M.NOOR PRIMARY & SECONDARY SCHOOL.

Schoolka waxa uu dugsi sare yeeshay 2017, asagoo dufcadii 1aad ee dhameesay 2020-2021. Taasi waxay sababtay inuu noqdo mid ka mid ah dugsiyada ugu guulaha badan deegaanka.
